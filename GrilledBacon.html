"Grilled Bacon Egg and Zuchini Boat"

2 medium zucchinis (about 1 pound 9 ounces total and/or each about 9 inches long)

One tablespoon of olive oil

Kosher Saky and Freshly Grounded Black Peper

1/4 cup of extra-sharp shredded cheddar

1/4 cup of grated Havarti cheese

1/4 cup of finely chopped fresh chives

1/4 Cup of heavy cream

4 large eggs

1 clove garlic grated

4 strips of bacon

Prepare a grill for medium-high indirect heat: For gas grills (with 3 or more burners), turn all the burners to medium-high heat; after about 15 minutes turn off the middle burner for 3-burner grills or the 2 middle burners for grills with 4 or more burners and keep the other 2 burners at medium high. For charcoal grills, bank one chimney starter-full of lit and ashed-over charcoal briquettes to one side of the grill. Set up a drip pan on the other side to avoid flare-ups. (The grill should be around 400 degrees F. Be sure to consult the grill manufacturers guide for the best results.)
